About


Mindy Carson grew up in Pennsylvania and attended Penn State University for Integrated Arts with a focus on Photgraphy, Metalsmithing, and Art History. After gradting Penn State, she completed an internship at the Fabric Workshop and Museum in Philadelphia, PA. Here she created yardage silk screen designs and volunteered at inner city schools to teach sreen printing with the museum.
She then moved to Asheville, NC and began her design career. She worked both independantly and as a design for a design house, Royal Peasantry. Here she focused on designing wearable leather pieces and jewelry.
In addition, she performed hair and make-up artistry for the Asheville Community Opera, The Montford Players acting troupe, Asheville Vaudeville, Seduction Sideshow Burlesque Troupe, and for multiple fashion shows and photoshoots with Royal peasantry, Vintage Moon and other local designers.
Mndy has also held position such as Production Assistant at Curious Sense Studios, and worked specifically on th Grateful Dead videogame. She has also been a Personal Assitant to an artist, managing gallery and personal affairs, and volunteered as educational coordinator at Ashevillage Institute, a sustainal living community that gives classes on cobb house construction and permaculture.
Since moving from Asheville to Seattle, she has worked at Anthropology and implemented window designs in two store locations for Anthro Earth Day, and has been an after schol teacher at Creative Coding for Kids, teaching kids to create and develop their own video games.
Currently she is attending a Software Developer program at Ada Developers Academy in Seattle Washington.
