{ coding beginnings }

April 2, 2016

A few months ago, I applied to Ada Developers Academy. It's an intense coding school for women that involves 7 months in the classroom learning how to code and making cool projects, and then 5 months at an internship with a tech company in or around Seattle. As you may have guessed, I was accepted!

So in February, I started learning to code with 23 other women, and it has been well, intense. And rewarding. I go to bed every night exhausted and often don't sleep very well, but I also wake up excited to go back and learn more.
