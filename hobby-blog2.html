Star Trek: The Sexed Generation - Day of the Holograms

The debut of our fully-scripted Star Trek burlesque parody show! This space-bound comic extravaganza tracks the crew of the Enterprise as they encounter the Borg, holograms gone wild, and more! Original comedy mixed with new and unique character-focused burlesque acts combined for an unforgettable trip to where no one has gone before!

Broadway Babes: A Burlesque Tribute to the Great White Way

This swingin’, sexy, and educational trip through the history of the intertwined histories of the musical and burlesque featured some of the best and most unique acts in town. Guided by host Sailor St. Claire, Broadway Babes was a cheeky combination of scholarly examination and raucous live example.

Star Trek: The Sexed Generation - The Space Hole Interdiction

The sequel to our first sold-out hit show, Star Trek: The Sexed Generation - Day of the Holograms, this episode of Star Trek: The Sexed Generation brings an all-new story with brand new characters, along with some returning favorites. A strange rift in space and time has sent the crew of the Enterprise tumbling into the past! Now they must work together with some unexpected allies in order to return to their own time!

Burl-X-Men: Days of Future Ass

This fully-scripted celebration of all things X-Men debuted to sold-out crowds. Two young women bond and fall in like over an action-packed issue of their favorite comic book, as the scenes of the story come to life! The X-Men are drawn to a tropical island in search of their leader, only to find more than they bargained for! How can they escape? Check out this exciting issue of X-Men to find out!

Revelry!

Our monthly revue show, Revelry takes place at Naked City Brewery in Greenwood on the first Monday of every month. A celebration of community and self-expression, Revelry is an opportunity to experience a fun, unique, intimate show in a relaxed environment. Throw in delicious food and house-brewed beer, and you have a monthly bash you won’t want to miss!
