Rock Climbing

I started rock climbing in college at the University of Delaware many moons ago. Since then I have learned sport and trad climbing, worked as a rock climbing trip leader, and climbed outdoors all over the country. Climbing has been a great way to stay in shape, keep social, take great outdoor trips, and constantly challenge myself. It can as much of a mental activity as a physical one sometimes and I love it.

Music/Crafting....I built this bass guitar.

There was a guitar building club at the high school I worked at in Brooklyn, so I was able to build this beauty and make it slightly smaller than a regular bass. I designed the shape, was able to customize my pickups, and get really good at sanding and oiling. About half the hours I spent working on this were just sanding it to get it juuuuuust right.
