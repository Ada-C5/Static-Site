Hi, my name is Nemesh.

(If these backgrounds are any indication, Sarah is pretty excited about me)

I'm an australian shepherd/golden retriever mix with lots of love and energy. April 1 was my second birthday - and no, I'm not joking! I really am that old!!!

Most often you can find me on the couch. I have an exhausting daily regimen of stretching and howling when my owners wake up, making Sarah uneasy on walks because I bark at other dogs, doing sumersaults on the couch, and chewing on walls. When I'm not on the couch, I can be found on the other couch.
