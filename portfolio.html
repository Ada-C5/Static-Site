Scrabble (pair programming)

(Mar 2016)

Project Summary:

Create the Scrabble board game! Players draw random tiles from a bag, play words, words are scored based on individual letter values (bonus points of all tiles in hand are played), and the first player to reach 100 points wins! Interactive board allows users to see what spaces are available and play a word wherever it fits.

Emphasis was placed on TDD and running specs using a Rakefile and the Minitest gem. 100% test coverage!

Languages:

Ruby
