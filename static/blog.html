Quote of the Day: Metz on Design

Design is more the art of preserving changeability than it is the act of achieving perfection.

Sandi Metz discussing software design in Practical Object-Oriented Ruby Design.

While reading Sandi Metz’s book on Ruby design I came across this quote in Chapter 2 and it has given me a new perspective on what my priorities are. I find I’m very often questioning myself about how potential changes in requirements will affect my design.
