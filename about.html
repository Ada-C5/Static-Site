about me

After working in the clinical research administration and compliance field for 10 years, I realized what I loved most about my positions revolved around learning how the software works, testing it, and finding ways to make it more useful for researchers. That led me to think about ways I could use those skills and love for technology to promote social change and advance public health.

Now a student at Ada Developers Academy, I'm studying software development over the course of a year. I'll be learning Ruby, Rails, HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript, and more. After seven months in the classroom, I'll spend five months as an intern at a local tech company.

When I'm not breaking my code, you can usually find me at a local craft brewery, reading non-fiction, getting together with friends for a board game, or planning my next travel adventure.
